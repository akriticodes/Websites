Bus Facilities and Services

The bus and coach industry in Nepal has come on leaps and bounds in recent years, and now many providers run modern fleets with comfortable, reclining seats, WiFi and air-conditioning for low rates. Further facilities and services can vary according to the bus provider. Many long-distance coaches will also offer comfort seating, which can be found on almost all providers. However, in some companies, you are likely to also find different service classes, refreshments, power sockets at each seat and entertainment services on some coaches. Long-haul trips will often provide scheduled stops at a variety of services along the route. Most bus providers offer a luggage policy, and if bringing any extra luggage or sports equipment, such as a bike, it is best to check with the company before booking as many buses will not carry a bicycle on international services.
