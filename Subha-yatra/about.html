About Us


Shubha Yatra, launched in 2018, is an online bus tickets booking system established with a goal to provide online bus ticketing services to everyone travelling by bus. Shubha Yatra provides information about price, availability and booking facility for buses through out Nepal. To make online bus booking process an easy affair for the people is an extremely important thing. Book bus tickets online, we offer bus ticket reservation online at lowest prices for all bus routes through out Nepal. Online bus tickets booking process is really simple within few minutes with few mouse clicks. One of the greatest advantages of Internet World, bus online booking is of immense use to busy travellers who don't have much time to book or purchase tickets via ticket counters.
